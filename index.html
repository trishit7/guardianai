          What GuardianAI solves
        

        

          Digital creators lose billions every year to image piracy, unauthorized reproduction, and AI-generated clones of their work. GuardianAI uses Google Gemini Vision AI to forensically compare two images and detect copyright infringement — and lets you proactively protect your assets with watermarking.
        

      


      

        

          
          How it works
        

        

          
1
Upload your original image (A) and the suspected copy (B)

          
2
Both images are sent to Gemini Vision AI with a forensic analysis prompt

          
3
Gemini returns similarity score, detected edits (cropping, color grading, mirroring), and a legal verdict

          
4
Use the watermark tab to protect your future assets before publishing
